How to Make a BLT

  1. Cook four or five slices of bacon.
  2. Slice Tomato.
  3. Chop Lettuce.
  4. Toast two slices of white bread (or preferred bread).
  5. Lightly spread mayo on both slices of bread.
  6. Assemble sandwich.
  7. Place bacon on one slice of bread.
  8. Add lettuce and tomato slices.
  9. Place other slice on top (mayo side down, of course)
  10. Enjoy sandwich with a crisp pickle on the side!
